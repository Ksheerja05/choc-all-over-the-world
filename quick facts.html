Quick facts about chocolate

  1. The U.S.A is the largest producer of chocolate (dark as well as normal).
  2. 65–75% of cocoa in chocolates is the most preferred range.
  3. Australia has the highest median ratings (3.5), while U.K and Ecuador have the smallest (3).
  4. Italy, Australia, and Switzerland have the best ratings for dark chocolate, and Canada and Ecuador for normal chocolate.
  5. Italy is the only country with ratings higher than 4 and it’s for dark chocolate.
  6. The liking for dark chocolate has increased over the years whereas it’s the same for normal chocolate.
  7. It takes 400 cocoa beans to make one pound of chocolate.
  8. Theobroma Cacao is the tree that produces cocoa beans, and it means “food of the gods.” Carolus Linnaeus, the father of plant taxonomy, named it.
  9. It takes two to four days to make a single-serving chocolate bar.
